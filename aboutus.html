About Us

A Rich Food Culture

India is a diverse, rich, and prosperous country. Among the richness of Indian culture, there was not only architecture, not only art and drama, not only science, not only spiritually, but also a rich food culture. Of course, all the organs of life were perfect. Our diet was written by our ancestors a few thousand years ago. This is wonderful.

Spices like black pepper, cinnamon, bay leaf, coriander, etc. were invented by Indians thousands of years ago. Later, with the help of these spices, different foods were made in India according to different geographical regions. These foods were extremely tasty. So even a few thousand years ago, foreign travelers were fascinated by our Indian cuisine.

By studying this, we have brought to you 12 flavors of traditional and modern tasty, nutritious Anantakoti Paratha Chivda...

2014

Anantkoti Enterprises was started from home in 2014. Initially the situation was very different as there was no material so I had to knead the flour by hand, cut, fry in a small basket as there was no pan for frying, I was using the stove as there was no grate.

We started our business with few resources, meager manpower, a shaky financial situation, and a lot of dreams in mind. From the name of the industry to the present day, we were faced with different challenges step by step and we were making our way through it, feeling, learning...

" Why not at the speed of a tortoise but walking."

2016-17

In 2017, we got an inspiration to go out of the house and take the machine to grow the industry and find the necessary licenses, logos, names, and space for the machine. We succeeded in trying to improve the quality. Then we bought machines for all the work of frying, rolling, kneading flour.

2018

Examining the market, we prepared a tasty, and equally nutritious Paratha Chivda for people of all ages. But after it was made, we performed all over Maharashtra to test how much people liked it and we kept getting return customers. It started with word of mouth marketing and we got an overwhelming response from the customers.

In June 2017, one of our customers took our Paratha Chivda for their US-based children and grandchildren, and we still remember their demand.Different customers were getting us due to the exhibition.

" .....and the journey became tastier."
